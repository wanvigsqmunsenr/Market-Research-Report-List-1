<p><p>The Artisan keycaps market comprises various types based on material and manufacturing methods. **Resin** keycaps are popular for their vibrant colors and custom designs but can be less durable. **SLA** (Stereolithography) keycaps are 3D printed for precision and detail, offering intricate designs but typically at a higher cost. **ABS** (Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene) keycaps are durable and widely used for mass production, providing a good balance of quality and affordability.
