<p><strong>The Auto Electromagnetic Compatibility Test Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bulk Current Injection (BCI)</li><li>Conducted Emissions (CE)</li><li>Conducted Transient Emissions (CTE)</li><li>Conducted Transient Immunity (CTI)</li><li>Electrostatic Discharge (ESD)</li><li>Radiated Emissions (RE)</li><li>Radiated Immunity (RI)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Auto Electromagnetic Compatibility Test Market encompasses various testing methods to ensure vehicle electronics function effectively in electromagnetic environments. Bulk Current Injection (BCI) assesses susceptibility to conducted disturbances. Conducted Emissions (CE) measures unwanted emissions from devices. Conducted Transient Emissions (CTE) evaluates brief, surging signals, while Conducted Transient Immunity (CTI) tests resistance to such disturbances. Electrostatic Discharge (ESD) measures tolerance against static electricity. Radiated Emissions (RE) examines emissions into the electromagnetic field, and Radiated Immunity (RI) assesses immunity to external electromagnetic fields.</p></p>
