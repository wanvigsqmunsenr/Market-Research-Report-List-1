<p><p>A dry granulator is a crucial piece of equipment used in the pharmaceutical, food, and chemical industries to convert powder into granules without the use of water or solvents. This process enhances the material properties, increasing flowability, compressibility, and stability. Dry granulation is typically achieved through processes like roller compaction or slugging, which can efficiently produce granules suitable for tablet formulation and other applications.</p><p>The Dry Granulator Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.3% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demand for high-efficiency production methods and the rising need for advanced manufacturing technologies in pharmaceuticals. Additionally, the growing trend towards green and sustainable processing is promoting the adoption of dry granulation techniques, which minimize moisture and waste. Innovations in dry granulator designs, such as increased automation and integration with other processes, are also enhancing productivity and operational efficiency. Furthermore, the expanding applications in industries like food and nutraceuticals are broadening market opportunities, indicating a robust potential for growth in the upcoming years. Overall, the dry granulator market is poised for significant advancements, meeting evolving industry demands effectively.</p></p>
