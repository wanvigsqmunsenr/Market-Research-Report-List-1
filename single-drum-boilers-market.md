<p><p>Single drum boilers are classified into horizontal and vertical types based on their design and orientation. Horizontal boilers feature a cylindrical shape oriented horizontally, allowing efficient heat transfer and steam production, making them suitable for various industrial applications. Vertical boilers, on the other hand, are designed to stand upright, occupying less floor space while still providing effective steam generation. Both types are used in processes requiring reliable steam supply, offering distinct advantages depending on space constraints and operational requirements.</p></p>
