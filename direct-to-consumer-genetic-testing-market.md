<p><p>The direct-to-consumer genetic testing market comprises various types, including diagnostic screening, preimplantation genetic diagnosis (PGD), and relationship testing. Diagnostic screening involves assessing genetic predispositions to diseases, allowing individuals to make informed health decisions. PGD is utilized in assisted reproductive technology, enabling prospective parents to identify genetic disorders in embryos before implantation. Relationship testing typically encompasses paternity tests, confirming biological connections between individuals. Each segment caters to consumer demand for personalized genetic insights, enhancing health awareness and reproductive choices.</p></p>
