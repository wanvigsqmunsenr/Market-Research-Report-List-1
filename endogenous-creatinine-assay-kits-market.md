<p><strong>The Endogenous Creatinine Assay Kits Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Jaffe's Kinetic Method</li><li>Enzymatic Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Endogenous Creatinine Assay Kits Market is primarily divided into two types: Jaffe's Kinetic Method and Enzymatic Method. The Jaffe's Kinetic Method relies on the reaction of creatinine with picric acid, resulting in a color change that is measured photometrically. In contrast, the Enzymatic Method employs specific enzymes to catalyze the conversion of creatinine into measurable products, offering higher specificity and reduced interference from other substances. Both methods are designed to accurately assess kidney function through creatinine levels.</p></p>
