<p><p>The noise barriers market comprises three main types: reflective, absorptive, and mixed. Reflective barriers deflect sound waves away from sensitive areas, making them suitable for urban environments. Absorptive barriers, constructed from materials that absorb sound, reduce noise levels by minimizing reflections, ideal for areas near highways or railways. Mixed barriers combine both reflective and absorptive properties, offering a balanced solution for various noise control needs. Each type serves specific applications in mitigating noise pollution effectively.</p></p>
