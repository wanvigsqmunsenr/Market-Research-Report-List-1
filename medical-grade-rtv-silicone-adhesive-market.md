<p><p>The medical grade RTV silicone adhesive market is segmented into two main types: single component and double component silicone adhesives. Single component silicone adhesives cure upon exposure to moisture in the air, offering convenience and ease of use, making them suitable for a variety of applications. In contrast, double component silicone adhesives require mixing two components before application, providing enhanced strength and durability. Both types are vital in medical applications, ensuring secure bonding for devices and prosthetics.</p></p>
