<p><strong>The Shipborne Automatic Identification System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Class A</li><li>Class B</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Shipborne Automatic Identification System (AIS) market is categorized into Class A and Class B systems. Class A is mandatory for larger commercial vessels, providing real-time tracking and communication for collision avoidance and maritime traffic management. It broadcasts vital information like vessel identity, position, and course. Class B, designed for smaller vessels and recreational boats, is voluntary, offering limited data and less frequent transmissions. Both systems enhance maritime safety but cater to different types of vessels and operational needs.</p></p>
