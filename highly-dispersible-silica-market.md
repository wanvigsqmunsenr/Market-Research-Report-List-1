<p><p>The highly dispersible silica market can be segmented based on form into two main types: granular and micropearls. Granular silica consists of larger particle sizes that provide excellent flowability and can enhance the mechanical properties of composites. In contrast, micropearls feature a fine, spherical particle shape, offering superior dispersion in various applications, especially in coatings and rubber products. Both forms are valued for enhancing performance characteristics and improving product consistency across industries like automotive, electronics, and personal care.</p></p>
