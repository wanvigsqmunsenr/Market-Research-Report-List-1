<p><ul><li>Scalar Magnetometer</li><li>Vector Magnetometer</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The generic magnetometer market consists of three primary types: scalar magnetometers, vector magnetometers, and others. Scalar magnetometers measure the strength of magnetic fields without regard to their direction, making them ideal for applications like mineral exploration. Vector magnetometers, on the other hand, sense both the magnitude and direction of magnetic fields, which is crucial for navigation and geophysical research. The "others" category includes specialized magnetometers tailored for specific applications such as industrial processes or environmental monitoring, broadening market versatility.</p></p>
