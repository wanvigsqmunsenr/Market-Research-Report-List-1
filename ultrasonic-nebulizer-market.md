<p><p>The ultrasonic nebulizer market comprises two main types: mechanical ultrasonic nebulizers and remote control ultrasonic nebulizers. Mechanical ultrasonic nebulizers utilize piezoelectric crystals to produce ultrasonic waves, effectively transforming liquid medication into fine mist for inhalation. In contrast, remote control ultrasonic nebulizers offer advanced functionality, allowing users to operate the device remotely, enhancing convenience and usability. Both types cater to diverse patient needs, particularly in respiratory therapies, with a focus on delivering precise aerosolized medication.</p></p>
