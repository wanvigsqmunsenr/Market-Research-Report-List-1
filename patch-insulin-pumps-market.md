<p><p>The patch insulin pump market consists of two primary types: closed-loop and open-loop systems. Closed-loop systems, often referred to as artificial pancreases, automatically adjust insulin delivery based on continuous glucose monitoring, providing precise control over blood sugar levels. In contrast, open-loop systems require users to manually input data for insulin dosing, offering flexibility but demanding more user interaction. Both types aim to improve diabetes management, with varied features to suit different patient needs and lifestyles.</p></p>
